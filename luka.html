Luka Doncic

Team: Los Angeles Lakers

Position: Guard / Forward

Height: 6'7"

Weight: 230 lbs

Nationality: Slovenian

NBA Debut: 2018

Awards: NBA Rookie of the Year (2019), 5× NBA All-Star, 5× All-NBA First Team

Fun Fact: Luka earned the nickname “Luka Magic” for his dazzling passes, step-backs, and clutch plays.

Biography

Luka Doncic, born February 28, 1999 in Ljubljana, Slovenia, is one of the most exciting young stars in the NBA today. Before joining the league, he starred with Real Madrid in the EuroLeague, where he won both the EuroLeague MVP and Final Four MVP at just 19 years old.

Drafted 3rd overall in 2018 (later traded to the Dallas Mavericks), Luka quickly became the face of the franchise. Known for his elite scoring, rebounding, and playmaking, Doncic is a nightly triple-double threat and one of the league’s most dynamic offensive forces.

Off the court, Luka is beloved for his charisma and humility. He remains deeply connected to his Slovenian roots and has inspired a new generation of international players to pursue NBA dreams.
