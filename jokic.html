Nikola Jokic

Team: Denver Nuggets

Position: Center

Height: 6'11"

Weight: 284 lbs

Nationality: Serbian

NBA Debut: 2015

Awards: 3× MVP (2021, 2022, 2024), NBA Champion & Finals MVP (2023), 7× NBA All-Star

Fun Fact: Jokic is known as "The Joker" and is considered one of the best passing big men in NBA history.

Biography

Nikola Jokic, born February 19, 1995 in Sombor, Serbia, is the cornerstone of the Denver Nuggets franchise. Drafted 41st overall in the 2014 NBA Draft, Jokic has defied expectations to become one of the most dominant players in the league. Known for his exceptional basketball IQ, court vision, and unselfish playmaking, Jokic revolutionized the center position by combining size and finesse with guard-like passing ability.

In the 2022–23 season, Jokic led the Denver Nuggets to their first ever NBA Championship, where he was named the Finals MVP. His ability to control the pace of the game, score at will, and elevate his teammates has made him one of the most unique superstars in basketball history.

Off the court, Jokic lives a quiet lifestyle and is passionate about horse racing, often spending his offseasons with his horses back in Serbia.
