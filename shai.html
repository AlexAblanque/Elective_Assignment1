Shai Gilgeous-Alexander

Team: Oklahoma City Thunder

Position: Guard

Height: 6'6"

Weight: 195 lbs

Nationality: Canadian

NBA Debut: 2018

Awards: NBA MVP (2025), NBA Finals MVP (2025), 4× NBA All-Star

Fun Fact: Shai is known for his smooth, methodical pace and unique fashion style, often being featured on best-dressed lists around the league.

Biography

Shai Gilgeous-Alexander, born July 12, 1998 in Toronto, Canada, has quickly risen to superstardom as one of the NBA’s premier guards. Drafted 11th overall in the 2018 NBA Draft, Shai’s combination of smooth ball handling, elite finishing ability, and efficient scoring has made him one of the toughest players to guard in today’s game.

In the 2024-2025 season, Shai cemented himself as a superstar by winning both the NBA MVP and the Finals MVP, leading the Oklahoma City Thunder to a championship. His calm demeanor and clutch performances in big moments have earned him respect across the league and among fans worldwide.

Off the court, Shai is admired for his unique sense of fashion, ranking among the NBA’s style icons. He also remains deeply connected to his Canadian roots, inspiring the next generation of basketball players in Canada.
