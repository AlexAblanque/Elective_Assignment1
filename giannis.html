Giannis Antetokounmpo

Team: Milwaukee Bucks

Position: Power Forward

Height: 6'11"

Weight: 242 lbs

Nationality: Greek-Nigerian

NBA Debut: 2013

Awards: 2× MVP (2019, 2020), NBA Champion & Finals MVP (2021), NBA Defensive Player of the Year (2020), 9× NBA All-Star

Fun Fact: Giannis is nicknamed the "Greek Freak" for his extraordinary athleticism and versatility.

Biography

Giannis Antetokounmpo, born December 6, 1994 in Athens, Greece, is the face of the Milwaukee Bucks and one of the NBA’s most dominant players. Drafted 15th overall in the 2013 NBA Draft, Giannis rapidly developed from a raw prospect into one of the greatest two-way players in basketball history. His mix of size, speed, and skill has earned him the nickname "Greek Freak."

In 2021, Giannis delivered one of the greatest Finals performances in NBA history, scoring 50 points in Game 6 to lead the Bucks to their first championship in 50 years. Known for his relentless drive, he continues to evolve his game, adding playmaking and leadership to his already unstoppable scoring and defense.

Off the court, Giannis is admired for his humility, humor, and dedication to his family. He has become an international icon and an inspiration to millions, representing the values of hard work and perseverance.
